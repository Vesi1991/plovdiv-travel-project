Why Plovdiv?

Plovdiv,the city of ancient history and Roman ruins.

Plovdiv is one of the oldest cities in Europe, with a history dating back over 6,000 years. It is famous for its Roman theater, cobbled Old Town, and colorful 19th-century houses. The city blends ancient heritage with a lively modern culture, making it a key cultural hub in Bulgaria.

Located in southern Bulgaria, Plovdiv is built around seven hills and offers stunning views and historic landmarks. It has been influenced by many civilizations, including the Thracians, Romans, Byzantines, and Ottomans. Today, it is known for its vibrant arts scene, museums, and festivals, attracting visitors from around the world.
